## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no login required. You get the full audit instantly.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price is verified against official vendor pricing pages with a
dated source. We update it regularly.

**Q: Do you store my data?**
A: Only if you choose to save your report via email. The audit itself
runs entirely in your browser.

**Q: What tools do you support?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, v0, Windsurf,
Anthropic API direct, and OpenAI API direct.

**Q: What is Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits sourced from
companies that overforecast. For high-savings audits, we surface Credex
as a way to capture even more of those savings.
